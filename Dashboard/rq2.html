Seasonal Patterns

Exploring monthly and yearly trends to identify seasonal patterns in traffic infractions.

Peak Season

December and January show 35% higher infraction rates, likely due to holiday travel and tourist activity.

Lowest Period

May through July consistently show the lowest infraction rates across all years analyzed.

Year-over-Year

Overall infraction rates have shown a steady increase of approximately 3-5% annually from 2019 to 2024.
